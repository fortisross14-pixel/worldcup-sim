# Alternate History Upgrade

This build adds:
- World Cup timeline beginning in 1930
- 16/24/32/48-team format progression
- weighted-random, non-historical era powers every five editions
- era-power boosts to team strength and star generation
- rarity-independent player career length
- tactical player roles and role/style chemistry
- expanded match engine with shots on target, xG, cards and offsides
- match quality ratings and match MVPs
- newspaper-style previews and result headlines
- sortable team, player and all-time game records
- tournament average quality and top-five games stored in history

Existing saves are migrated lazily: missing year, format and era fields are generated when loaded or when the next World Cup begins.
