# Latest corrections

- Fixed mobile match headers so team names are not covered by the central score card.
- Player tactical roles now appear in previews, post-match ratings, records and GOAT profiles.
- Added a newspaper-style World Cup review after the final, covering the top scorer, tournament MVP and repeat-MVP records, favorite disappointments and the tournament's best game.
- Recalibrated match quality. A 10/10 is restricted to semi-finals/finals featuring a legendary or generational player, six or more goals, a major comeback, a one-goal margin and at least 24 shots.
- Rebuilt GOAT scoring around games, goals, match MVPs, tournament MVPs, Golden Boots, career average rating and team finishes. Titles are valuable; being the leading performer on winning teams creates an additional bonus, while careers without deep team success are discounted.
- Save compatibility retained; missing new fields are calculated from stored history where possible.
